About Me


Hello, my name is Andrew and this is my vision of the world!
I am an artist that is fasincated by color and reflection. If you notice on my photos, I typically aim for lots of color illumination and a reflective element, be it with water or simply using my phone. There is something about LEDs that fasincate me so much and how it works so I try to implement it into my works in whatever way I can. I also love the sense of adventure because it reminds me of being a kid from Calvin and Hobbes which I value whole hearty because it gives me a sense of no limit restriction, which is why I am able to produce the works I create now.

I was born and raised in San Jose, California. Have been here my whole life so far, but hopefully I can take my works elsewhere in the Bay. Either up north in San Fransico or south to Los Angeles. I hope to pursure in the works of web development, graphic design, photography, and cinematography.
