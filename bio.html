I come from San Jose, California and base my work off a lot of the LED projects around the Bay Area. The use of neon lights within the area has become my source of lighting and is incorporated into my photography a lot. I tend to investigate the ability to take photos in poor lighting conditions as most people stray away from that. My other interests include of coding in HTML/CSS as well as doing graphic designs. I am currently studying at SJSU with a Digital Media Art major.
